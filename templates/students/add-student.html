Step 2: Fill Your Data
Required Fields:
  • First Name*, Last Name*, Student ID*, Admission Number* - Must be unique
  • Gender* (Male/Female/Other) - Use full words (Male, Female, Other)
  • Date of Birth* (YYYY-MM-DD) - Format: 2000-01-15
  • Email* - Must be unique email address
  • Batch Name*, Semester Number*, Section Name* - These must match existing records
  • Address* - Student's residential address
Important Notes:
  • Student ID and Email must be unique - duplicate entries will be rejected
  • Batch, Semester, Section must already exist in the system
  • Parent fields are optional
  • Date format must be exactly: YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2005-06-15)
  • Do not change the column headers in the template
